Homebrewing

Having two great-great-grandfathers who were brewers in Chicago and Buffalo, I discovered a rarely used brewing kit and began brewing my own beer following the trail of my ancestors. I started with the basic liquid malt extracts and moved to clone kits with grains, hops, and dry malt extract. My latest beer is a recipe I devised based on the various clone kits I've brewed. I call it El D for the El Dorado hops used.
